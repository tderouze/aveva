## Description
Description: "In this presentation Christiane Billing from Bayer will be presenting a robust blueprint of how Bayer is leveraging production data to secure their future in industrial excellence.

Bayer’s manufacturing enablement team has developed an ecosystem of technology and approaches that empower their operation’s teams to enhance capacity and efficiency with real sustainable impact.

This session will underscore the power of integrating AVEVA’s PI solutions and TrendMiner and successfully spreading predictive analytics, real-time monitoring and cross-functional collaboration across the global production sites. Demonstrating the efficacy of the blueprint by concluding the session with tangible examples of their recent accomplishments and outlining their future strategic plan."
